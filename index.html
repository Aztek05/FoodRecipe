Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with pancetta. Turn off the heat and pour in the egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Toss quickly, adding reserved pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
